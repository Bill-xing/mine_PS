# Applicant Fact Bank - Fall 2027 Master's Personal Statements

This document is the auditable fact source for the Fall 2027 personal-statement portfolio. It records applicant history, safe quantitative claims, and applicant-approved narrative framing. It is not program research and it excludes private contact details. Mutable admissions thresholds and program inclusion or exclusion decisions are owned by program_verification_matrix.md, not this applicant fact bank.

## Source hierarchy

Use sources in this order:

1. Final English resume PDF and its Typst source.
2. Final Chinese resume PDF and its TeX source.
3. Personal website source, limited to biography, project, internship, and award source files.
4. Project repositories or technical reports when they define the implementation or evaluation context of a resume claim. For quantitative claims, the underlying evaluation record controls metric definitions and comparison validity.
5. Existing personal-statement and EasierPS drafts only as discovery leads. A draft sentence is not evidence unless a higher-priority source supports it.

Applicant-approved goals are a separate evidence class. They may guide narrative framing but must not be presented as completed history or third-party-verified fact.

Technical evaluation records control metric definitions and comparison validity and may narrow or invalidate resume shorthand. The Chinese resume or technical source may add context but may not silently alter verified history. The following discrepancies are resolved:
